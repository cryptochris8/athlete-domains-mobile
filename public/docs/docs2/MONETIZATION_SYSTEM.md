# Monetization System

## Overview
This system defines how the app generates revenue through:
- Avatar packs
- Coin economy
- In-app purchases
- Rewarded ads

## Currency
### Coins (Soft Currency)
Earned via:
- Gameplay
- Wins
- Daily rewards
- Ads

Used for:
- Buying packs
- Upgrading avatars

## Pack System
### Basic Pack
- Cost: 500 coins
- Contains: 1 avatar
- Odds:
  - Common: 70%
  - Rare: 25%
  - Epic: 4%
  - Legendary: 1%

### Pro Pack
- Cost: 1500 coins OR $0.99
- Contains: 3 avatars
- Guarantee: 1 Rare+

### Elite Pack
- Cost: $2.99
- Contains: 5 avatars
- Guarantee: 1 Epic+

### Legendary Pack
- Cost: $4.99
- Contains:
  - 1 Legendary
  - 2 additional avatars

## In-App Purchases
### Coin Packs
- $0.99 → 1000 coins
- $2.99 → 4000 coins
- $4.99 → 8000 coins

### Starter Pack
- $0.99
- 10 avatars
- Guaranteed 2 Rare+

### Remove Ads
- $1.99
- Removes all ads permanently

## Ads
Rewarded ads only:
- Double coins
- Free pack
- Retry
- Daily boost
